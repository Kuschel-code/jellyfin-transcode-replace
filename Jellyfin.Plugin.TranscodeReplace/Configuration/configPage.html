Verification

A re-encode is always checked (codec, duration, audio tracks, size) before it replaces anything. VMAF adds a perceptual-quality gate.
95 ≈ visually transparent; below ~90 the loss is visible. An encode that scores below the threshold is rejected and the original is kept.

Replacement

How the original is handled once an encode passes every check.
Hard replace overwrites the original immediately with no backup. A later disk failure or a bad encode is unrecoverable. Strongly consider “Backup, then delete” plus the VMAF gate.
